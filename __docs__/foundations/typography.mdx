# Typography

## Shape

<img src={shapeImg} alt="Shape" />

### Typography

Our typographic system is built on legibility and accessibility. It's clear, consistent, and approachable — giving structure to every surface without overshadowing the content.

## Principles

<PrincipleSet>
    <Principle
        label="Structured"
        title="Hierarchy over decoration"
        description="Every type choice should create structure, not style. If a decision can't be explained by the hierarchy, it shouldn't be there."
    />
    <Principle
        label="Accessible"
        title="Legibility at every level"
        description="From 12px metadata to 32px headlines, type must be readable for every learner — regardless of age, context, or device."
    />
    <Principle
        label="Consistent"
        title="Consistency builds clarity"
        description="The same style should always mean the same thing. When two styles look identical, they should behave identically."
    />
    <Principle
        label="Intentional"
        title="Let content lead"
        description="Type should recede so understanding can happen. When in doubt, use less."
    />
</PrincipleSet>

## Plus Jakarta Sans

<img src={plusJakartaSansImg} alt="Plus Jakarta Sans" />

### Typeface - Plus Jakarta Sans

Chosen for its clarity, modern aesthetic, and approachable character. Plus Jakarta Sans is highly legible across all platforms — its clean lines and balanced forms create a consistent, trustworthy visual voice for every audience.
