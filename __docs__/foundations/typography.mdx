# Typography

## Shape

<img src={shapeImg} alt="Shape" />

### Typography

Our typographic system is built on legibility and accessibility. It's clear, consistent, and approachable — giving structure to every surface without overshadowing the content.

## Principles

Structured

### Hierarchy over decoration

Every type choice should create structure, not style. If a decision can't be explained by the hierarchy, it shouldn't be there.

Accessible

### Legibility at every level

From 12px metadata to 32px headlines, type must be readable for every learner — regardless of age, context, or device.

Consistent

### Consistency builds clarity

The same style should always mean the same thing. When two styles look identical, they should behave identically.

Intentional

### Let content lead

Type should recede so understanding can happen. When in doubt, use less.

### Typeface - Plus Jakarta Sans

Chosen for its clarity, modern aesthetic, and approachable character. Plus Jakarta Sans is highly legible across all platforms — its clean lines and balanced forms create a consistent, trustworthy visual voice for every audience.
